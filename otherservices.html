Our Appliance Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Contact us to receive a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your needs. Once you're happy with the quote, we’ll schedule a convenient time for your appliance cleaning. On the day of your appointment, our team will provide a courtesy call to confirm we’re on the way, ensuring you’re always prepared for our arrival.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, we conduct a thorough inspection of your appliance to identify any issues and ensure all components are in good condition. We take extra precautions to protect your floors and surrounding areas, using protective materials to prevent any mess or damage during the cleaning process.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We carefully disassemble removable parts, where applicable, such as filters, trays, or racks, and soak them in our specialized cleaning solutions. While these components are treated to remove stubborn grease and grime, we focus on the appliance interior, using eco-friendly methods to eliminate buildup and restore its original condition.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once all areas are thoroughly cleaned, we polish surfaces for a refreshed, like-new finish. We then reassemble the appliance, ensuring each component is securely in place and fully functional. Our team’s attention to detail guarantees a flawless result for every service.

Step 5: Final Touches

After reassembling your appliance, we perform a final inspection to ensure it’s spotless and working properly. We tidy up the workspace, leaving your home as clean as we found it. With your appliance looking and functioning at its best, you can enjoy its renewed efficiency and appearance with ease.
